Nuwara Eliya Vegetable Farms

Known as "Little England," Nuwara Eliya is famous for its cool climate and fertile soil, making it ideal for vegetable farming. The region produces a variety of vegetables, including carrots, leeks, cabbages, and potatoes. The farms here often offer tours where visitors can learn about organic farming practices and purchase fresh produce directly from the source.

Dambulla Agriculture Farm

Dambulla is a key agricultural hub in Sri Lanka, known for its extensive vegetable cultivation. The Dambulla Dedicated Economic Center is a major wholesale market for vegetables, where farmers from across the region sell their produce. The farms in Dambulla are renowned for growing a wide range of vegetables such as eggplants, tomatoes, and green beans, benefiting from the region's favorable climate and irrigation

Ella Organic Farms

Nestled in the picturesque hills of Ella, these organic farms focus on sustainable and eco-friendly farming practices. The area is not only popular for its beautiful landscapes and tourism but also for its organic vegetable farms. Visitors can explore the farms, participate in farming activities, and enjoy the fresh, organic produce, including leafy greens, peppers, and herbs, which are often supplied to local markets and restaurants.
